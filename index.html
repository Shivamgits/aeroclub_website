Flight Simulation

Experience the thrill of flying with our aeromodelling club's flight simulation program.

Learn the basics of flying and hone your skills with realistic simulations of various aircraft and conditions. Join us for regular training sessions and take your aeromodelling skills to new heights.

Drones

Unlock a new dimension of aeromodelling with our drone program. Learn how to build, program and fly your own drones, and participate in exciting drone racing and obstacle course challenges.

Explore the world from a new perspective and discover the many practical applications of drones in fields such as agriculture, search and rescue, and aerial photography.

RC Planes

Our aeromodelling club is excited to introduce our new RC plane program. These remote-controlled planes offer a unique and thrilling flying experience for all skill levels.

In addition to flying, our program also includes workshops and training sessions where members can learn about building and maintaining their own RC planes.
